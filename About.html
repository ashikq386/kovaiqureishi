ABOUT US

Guiding pilgrims with care & devotion

For over fifteen years, Kovai Qureishi has helped thousands of pilgrims from Coimbatore and across India undertake the sacred journeys of Hajj and Umrah with dignity and peace of mind.

OUR STORY

Built on trust, one journey at a time

What began as a small family endeavour has grown into one of the region's most trusted Hajj and Umrah operators. Our founders made these journeys themselves, and built Kovai Qureishi to give every pilgrim the guidance, comfort and reassurance they wished they'd had.

Today, our experienced scholars and on-ground teams accompany every group — handling the logistics so you can focus entirely on your worship.
