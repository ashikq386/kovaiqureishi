WHAT IS UMRAH

A journey of the heart, at any time of year

Umrah is a sacred visit to the Holy Kaaba in Makkah that can be undertaken at any time. Though not obligatory like Hajj, it is a deeply rewarding act of worship — a chance to seek forgiveness, renew faith, and draw closer to Allah. Our team makes the experience smooth, dignified and unforgettable for first-time and returning pilgrims alike.

THE RITES OF UMRAH

Four steps, fully guided

01

Ihram

Enter the sacred state of Ihram and make your intention (niyyah) before reaching the Miqat.

02

Tawaf

Circle the Holy Kaaba seven times in devotion, beginning at the Black Stone.

03

Sa'i

Walk seven times between the hills of Safa and Marwah, following Hajar's footsteps.

04

Halq / Taqsir

Shave or trim the hair to complete your Umrah and exit the state of Ihram.
