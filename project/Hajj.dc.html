WHAT IS HAJJ

Once in a lifetime, upon those who are able

Hajj is the fifth pillar of Islam and an obligation, once in a lifetime, for every Muslim who is physically and financially able. Performed during the month of Dhul Hijjah, it unites millions of pilgrims in a profound act of unity, devotion and submission to Allah.

THE DAYS OF HAJJ

A guided day-by-day journey

8 DHUL HIJJAH
Mina

Enter Ihram and travel to Mina for a day of prayer and reflection.

9 DHUL HIJJAH
Arafat

The day of Arafah — standing in supplication, the essence of Hajj.

10 DHUL HIJJAH
Muzdalifah

Stoning of the Jamarat, sacrifice, and Eid al-Adha celebration.

11–12 DHUL HIJJAH
Tashreeq

Days of remembrance in Mina with continued stoning of the Jamarat.

13 DHUL HIJJAH
Tawaf al-Wada

The farewell Tawaf, completing your Hajj before departure.
